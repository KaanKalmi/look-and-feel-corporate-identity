Who are the 5 stakeholders?

1. The environment

The environment is affected by your business because of the resource usage from the environment to make your products. Your business also produces waste which is released into the environment.

2. The staff

The staff are affected by your business because they are the ones who are working for you. They are the ones who are making your products and are the ones who are selling your products.

3. The owners and financers

The owners and financers are affected by your business because they are the ones who are funding your business. They are the ones who are making sure that your business is running smoothly.

4. The suppliers

The suppliers are affected by your business because they are the ones who are supplying you with the resources to make your products. They are the ones who are making sure that you have the resources to make your products.

5. The customers

The customers are affected by your business because they are the ones who are buying your products. They are the ones who are making sure that your business is running smoothly.
